Your safety is at risk

Current safety systems at large-scale festivals are flawed.s

With almost 10,000 attendees at Trinidad and Tobago's world-famous Carnival celebrations, the vast and uncontrolled crowds contain major safety risk factors such as sexual assaults, dehydration, and significant loss of connectivity between loved ones during the event.

Accessibility and efficiency are key, and without a way to pinpoint a person in need and get to them quickly, the hundreds of thousands of people attending the Carnival are at risk.

Next

Our Solution

MateMap Implements an Emergency Localization System, which is activated through a mobile application. Once activated, attendees can add other attendees in their friendlist. This enables them to keep track the location of their friends through the interactive map on the mobile app. The interactive map also includes key landmarks such as first aid booth and water stations.

The mobile app is packed with a panic button feature. Once pressed, the app will notify the security team with your location that you are in danger. This feature will also alert your friends and give them your current location.

Next

How It Works

The science behind it

MateMap takes advantage of latest Bluetooth® 4.0 technology. Receivers are located around the proximity of the Carnival site. It listens for signals transmitted from the app of nearby devices. The time, the location, and the identification can be identified by the receiver. With the Emergency Localization System, you are forever protected.
